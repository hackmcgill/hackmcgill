HackMcGill is beginner-friendly. We offer hands-on help for people with little to no experience in programming at our Learn to Code events.

Improve your skills

Come to Hack 101 to learn about the things you wish you would learn about in class. In these tutorials, we guide you through cool APIs to text you the weather on mars or how to build a web backend.

Discover projects that others are working on, and maybe collaborate on a cool hack. Perhaps you'll form a hackathon dream team. Optimize your friend-making and join our Facebook group !
