Join HackMcGill's Organizing Team


HackMcGill is McGill’s student hacker collective, most known for our organization of McGill’s largest annual hackathon, McHacks.

We’re looking for ambitious and friendly individuals passionate about tech to join our organizing team for the 2022-2023 school year. You’ll find descriptions of the roles we’re looking to fill below.

All organizers are required to attend weekly all hands meetings and team standups during the school year and over the summer. The average time commitment for organizers are 5-10 hours/week, though exact time commitment varies by team and is dependent on time of year.

We are currently recruiting for a few positions. The application deadline is Wednesday, September 21st at 11:59 PM. Good luck!
